Abdominal Pain

Abdominal Pain is a common complaint that can be caused by wide range of Conditions. It can be occur in any part of of the abdomen , and intensity of pain can range from mild to severe.


Possible Causes Of Abdominal Pain


Gastrointestinal disorders: bThis includes conditions such as indigestion, heartburn, constipation, diarrhea, irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), and inflammatory bowel disease (IBD).


Infections: Abdominal pain can be caused by infections in the digestive tract, such as food poisoning or a stomach virus.


Reproductive disorders: Women may experience abdominal pain due to menstrual cramps, ovarian cysts, or other reproductive issues.


Hernias: A hernia occurs when an organ or tissue protrudes through a weak spot in the abdominal muscles. This can cause pain, especially when the person is physically active or coughing.


Appendicitis: This is a serious condition that occurs when the appendix becomes inflamed and requires surgical removal. It is a common cause of abdominal pain, especially in young people.


Kidney stones: Small, hard deposits that form in the kidneys can cause abdominal pain if they become stuck in the urinary tract.
