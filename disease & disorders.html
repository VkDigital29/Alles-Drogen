Covid - 19 (SARS CoV - 2)

Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus 2 is a strain of Coronavirus that causes COVID-19.

Firstly it was identified in Wuhan , China. WHO declared the outbreak of COVID-19 on January 30 , 2020, and as Pandemic on March 11 , 2020.

It is a positive sense single-strained RNA Virus that is contagious in humans.

Symptoms :-

Possible symptoms include :

  • Fever or Chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty in breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Sure Throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea

This List does not include all Possible symptoms.
