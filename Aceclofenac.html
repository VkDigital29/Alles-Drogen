Aceclofenac

Aceclofenac is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID)
Analog of diclofenac.
It is used for the relief of pain and inflammation in rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis
and ankylosing spondylitis.
